Diversity in Tech Scholarship Submission

January 2023

About

Upon applying for my Coder Academy web development bootcamp, my immediate plan was to apply for the Diversity in Tech Scholarship. The brief for the scholarship application was quite broad - we had to submit a creative portfolio using our choice of media to convey who we were, and how we would contribute to increasing diversity in the tech industry.

I knew that with my submission, I wanted to challenge myself and show was I was capable of. I had some previous experience with web languages such as HTML and CSS, though this was going to require a lot of time and research to successfully deliver my vision.

Over the course of a two week period (around my work and sport commitments), I was able to build a basic and deploy a basic website from scratch, without formal education in best practice. My appreciation of clean, adaptive design ensured that I considered media breakpoints from the very beginning and constructed hand-drawn wireframes for both mobile and web views. I am also incredibly grateful to be surrounded by intelligent and helpful peers, who could provide me with tips and assist in testing for any display errors that could be cleaned up.

Skills and technologies used:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • VS Code
  • GitHub
  • Cloudflare
